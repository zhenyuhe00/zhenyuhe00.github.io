# About me

I am a third-year PhD student at Peking University, advised by Prof. [Di He](https://dihe-pku.github.io/). Currently, I am an intern at ByteDance Seed working on coding agents. I have also previously interned at Microsoft Research Asia and DP Technology. I earned my bachelor's degree at UESTC.

## Recent Focus

My recent work focuses on enhancing the coding agent capabilities of LLMs. Selected projects include:

1. 2025.9~Now: I am the core contributor to the 01 web generation capabilities of the Seed Code 2.0 model series. Collaborating with several great colleagues, we built a complete and mature optimization pipeline from scratch for 01 web generation, enabling continuous iteration to enhance model performance through SFT data synthesis, RL optimization, reward modeling, and novel in-house innovations.

2. 2025.2~2025.8: Led the development of SWE-Swiss, a 32B open-weight model that achieves top-tier performance on SWE-bench Verified.
